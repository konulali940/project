About Virtual Internship Program

Objectives

The Virtual Internship Program was launched in October 2020.The purpose of the virtual internship program is to provide young people with the opportunity to gain interactive experience in their field or other areas in a limited environment, to help them develop skills and self-confidence to ensure success in their future careers, to support the formation of qualified personnel in society.
